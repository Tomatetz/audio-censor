# Sound sample attribution

The WAV files in this directory are edited excerpts: converted to mono 48 kHz
PCM, trimmed, faded, filtered, and loudness-normalized.

## Bark samples

- `bark_1.wav`, `bark_2.wav`: excerpts from
  [Barking of a dog](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Barking_of_a_dog.ogg),
  Amada44, CC BY-SA 3.0.
- `bark_3.wav`: excerpt from
  [Barking of a dog 2](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Barking_of_a_dog_2.ogg),
  Amada44, CC BY-SA 3.0.
- `bark_4.wav`: excerpt from
  [Perro ladrando](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Perro_ladrando.ogg),
  Armartinell, CC BY-SA 4.0.

## Meow samples

- `meow_1.wav`: excerpt from
  [Meow of a Siamese cat](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Meow_of_a_Siamese_cat_-_freemaster2.wav),
  freemaster2, CC0.
- `meow_2.wav`: excerpt from
  [Meow of a pleading cat](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Meow_of_a_pleading_cat.oga),
  Heismark, public domain.
- `meow_3.wav`: excerpt from
  [Maullido de gata hembra joven](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Maullido_de_gata_hembra_joven.ogg),
  George Miquilena, CC0.
- `meow_4.wav`: excerpt from
  [GettingOutImpatient](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:GettingOutImpatient.ogg),
  Heismark, public domain.
